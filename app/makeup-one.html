The peculiarity of wedding makeup is that it should emphasize the beauty of the bride during the day and in the evening. When creating the image of the bride, it is important to consider many nuances. Makeup should emphasize the beauty of the hairstyle, outfit, combined with the color of hair and eyes.

For wedding makeup, you should prefer permanent makeup. This is due to the fact that the bride's image should remain flawless until late in the evening, because there is usually no opportunity to correct the makeup at the wedding. In addition, during the festive event there can be a rush of emotions, which should not spoil the appearance.
